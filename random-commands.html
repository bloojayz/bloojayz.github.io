Here are some Miscellaneous commands if you are making a survival server or anything like that:

/scoreboard teams add

/scoreboard teams option red color red

/scoreboard teams option blue color blue

/scoreboard teams option green color green

/scoreboard teams option gray color gray

/scoreboard teams option black color black

/scoreboard teams option orange color orange

/scoreboard teams option yellow color yellow

/scoreboard teams option white color white

 

/scoreboard teams join red @r[team=,c=1]

/scoreboard teams join blue @r[team=,c=1]

/scoreboard teams join green @r[team=,c=1]

/scoreboard teams join gray @r[team=,c=1]

/scoreboard teams join black @r[team=,c=1]

/scoreboard teams join gold @r[team=,c=1]

/scoreboard teams join @r[team=,c=1]

/scoreboard teams join @r[team=,c=1]

/spreadplayers 0 0 200 254 true @a

 

This adds a bunch of teams, and then automatically makes the players joins the teams, hence the second part. Then it spreads out everyone randomly in a large square. These are mostly just going to be used for an UHC map, but I thought that I would include it anyways.
