Lets just go straight into my Season 6 Jax Guide.

 

Jax can go either top or jungle. Most people prefer him as a top laner though.

 

 

 

SUMMONERS:

 

Top:                                                         OR                                                                             Jungle:


 

 

 

Both options are pretty viable. Use teleport if you are playing against a counter or if you need more lane pressure somewhere else. Otherwise, ignite is stronger. Flash is a mandatory summoner spell as you can use it to be really aggressive and you can use it to escape from from ganks.

ABILITIES

 

Top:

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

You want to start with your E, then into Q before maxing out w to optimize damage in fights. After that you want to max out your Q for the lower cooldown before going into your E.

 

If you are against a ranged top laner, then go for a Q max for the mini nuke.

 

If you are jungling, the path is about the same. Just go E first, then W, THEN Q, and continue with the top lane ability path.

 

 

                      Relentless Assault

                       Jax's passive increases his attack speed by a ton. If you can control this and keep at 6 stacks, then trading with your enemy will become a breeze. Always ramp up your

                       passive before you all in. Make sure to always try to keep the buff up by attacking at the last moment.

 

 

 

                        Leap Strike

                        This is your gap closer. Use it to hop over walls onto wards. Always max this spell first if you against a ranged because the cooldown goes down like crazy so you can

                         continuously jump on their faces.

 

                        Laning: Auto twice for your ult buff. Then Q onto your target and auto, then W to reset the auto and hit them again. This is Jax's standard trade.

 

                   Jungle: Use this spell only as a gap closer. If they dont have flash, then you can use your E to stun them as you land onto the target. And a handy trick to increase your

                                     jungle clear times by a little: If you have a blue buff, you can q to a minion if you are very close to it to reset your auto attack and deal a bit extra damage.

 

                        Late Game: This spell is just a handy gap closer. It is also very handy to escape.

 

 

                       Empower

                       This ability is pretty much only used for resetting your autoattack timer. Use it with your ult proc to deal a bunch of extra damage.

 

 

 

 

 

                      Counter Strike

                      Use this spell to dodge crap. It is very good when you leave it at level 1 so you don't really have to upgrade the spell. Make sure to always try to use the full duration of

                      this spell to get the most of it.

 

                     Laning: Use this spell when you engage people. Also use when you disengage after you Q to them and need to get out of the situation

 

                      Jungle: Good use of this spell to dodge to autos from the monsters to get the most of the ability. To dodge to attacks, use your E as the monster begins its attack

                                  animation.

 

                      Ganking: Walk up to your target while spinning your E and then stun. Then walk ahead of your target and beat him up

 

                      Late Game: Use this spell to group stun and when you are dueling an auto attack reliant champ (ADC)

 

                      Grandmaster's Might

                      Pretty good ability, the proc is a great way to burst the other guy down. Also use to not take extra damage like when you are towerdiving or dueling or crap like that :)
