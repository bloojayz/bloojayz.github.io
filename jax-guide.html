Lets just go straight into my Jax Guide.

 

Jax can go either top or jungle. Most people prefer him as a top laner though.

 

If you are going top, your summoners should probably be flash and teleport/ignite. This combo works pretty well with him

 

If you are going Jungle, the only way to go is flash and smite. Any other combo doesn't really work.
